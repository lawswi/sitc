SPACE IN THE CITY

WINCHESTER, ENGLAND

January 2020

Angela Tilby

Canon Angela Tilby addressed the idea of sin in the Bible and the Christian spiritual tradition, and what sense we can make of spiritual warfare.

29 January and 5 & 12 February 2020, 12.30 – 1pm
at The United Church, Jewry Street, Winchester
29 January What’s wrong with us?  Metaphors and images of sin and their resonance today.
