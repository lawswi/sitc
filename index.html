SPACE IN THE CITY

WINCHESTER, ENGLAND

Space in the City talks are on hold pending government and church guidelines for keeping such meetings safe for those who attend.

Meanwhile, the archive of previous series is below.  Enjoy one of the series from the past and hear the likes of … Bishop Richard Harries, Paula Gooder, David Winter, James Runcie or Salley Vickers … giving their talks in recent years.

Space in the City is an ecumenical venture organised by lay, licensed, ordained and associate members of Winchester’s city churches:  St Bartholomew and St Lawrence with St Swithun-upon-Kingsgate, St Peter’s and the United Church.
